BMGT407 - INFORMATION SYSTEMS PROJECTS
BMGT407 is the "capstone" course of the Information Systems (IS) major. The goal of the course is to provide the students with opportunity to apply the knowledge and skills that they have acquired in other IS courses through developing IS solutions to solve real world business problems
